Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their information collected is being used by us. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our app.



What personal information do we collect from the people who use our app?

We ask permission for accessing photos, media and files on your device and your personal details such as Name, Mobile Number & Device ID.


When do we collect information?

We collect information from you when you install our app and the personal details are collected when you participate in the Spinner Wheel on the app.


How do we use your information?

We may use the information we collect from you when you install the app or use the app features in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content and product offerings in which you are most interested.
  • To allow you to share the offers with others from your mobile device
  • To allow you to participate in the random draw for the Spinner Wheel

Do we collect Device related and Log Data?

We may collect device related information like OS version, Device IDs and IP address for usage analytics and for setting up audit trail and user identification and authentication checks for the transactions carried out. This will also include information relating to location and time of login, number of access made and duration and pages visited. Log file information is automatically reported by the app each time the user makes a request to access (i.e., visit) various sections of the app. Our servers automatically record certain log file information, including the users web request, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, referring / exit pages and URLs, number of clicks and how the user interacts with links on the app, pages viewed, and other such information. The information allows for more accurate reporting and improvement of the service.
